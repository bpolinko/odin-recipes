Hot Dirty Bird Grilled Wings

Quick, easy, delicious wings, whats not to love?!

These Hot Dirty Bird wings are the perfect level of spice. Cooked using a Komado Joe, these will be the only wings you ever want to eat moving forward!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken wings
  • Cosmo Q's Hot Dirty Bird Seasoning

Steps

  1. Pat the chicken dry with a paper towel
  2. Toss chicken in seasoning blend
  3. Start grill with deflector plate on one side
  4. Once grill is at temp, place wings over indirect heat, close dome, and cook for 25 minutes
  5. Move wings to direct heat, remove from grill once at desired crispiness
