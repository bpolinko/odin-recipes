Fancy Instant Ramen

Turn 50c Ramen into a classy meal!

Once you have tried this elevated ramen, youll never go back to just noodles and broth!

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of ramen
  • Chicken or pork belly, seasoned and cooked
  • Canned corn
  • 1 boiled egg
  • Nori
  • Green onion, chopped

Steps

  1. Cook ramen per directions on bag
  2. Cut boiled egg in half
  3. Pour ramen into bowl, and top with ingredients
  4. Serve and enjoy
